# 5.1 BIG Idea Statement

## 🎯 Цель

Сформировать финальную BIG Idea articulation.

**Время:** 2-4 часа  
**После:** BIG Idea statement + supporting doc

---

## 📋 Что такое BIG Idea Statement?

**Простыми словами:** BIG Idea Statement = краткая, мощная формулировка твоей продуктовой идеи.

**Цель:** За 2-3 страницы объяснить:
- Что делаем?
- Для кого?
- Почему это важно?
- Как монетизируем?

---

## 📝 Process

### Step 1: Articulate BIG Idea (1-2 hours)

**Template:**
```markdown
## BIG Idea Statement

**BIG Idea: [2-3 sentence summary]**

**For** [target segment],
**Who** [JTBD - functional + emotional jobs],
**The** [product/solution] **is a** [product category],
**That** [key value proposition].

**Unlike** [alternatives/competitors],
**We** [primary differentiation/moat].

**We'll validate this by** [validation approach]
**and achieve** [mNSM + revenue target] **within** [timeframe].
```

**Пример:**
```markdown
## BIG Idea Statement

**BIG Idea: AI-powered market research for SMB marketers — automated insights at 1/10th traditional cost.**

**For** SMB marketing teams (1-10 people),
**Who** need rapid audience insights but can't afford enterprise research,
**SparkToro is** an AI market research platform,
**That** provides audience data in minutes, not weeks.

**Unlike** expensive agencies or DIY surveys requiring expertise,
**We** automate research with AI at $49/month (10x cheaper).

**We'll validate this by** landing 5,000 active users within 6 months,
**and achieve** $500k ARR driven by freemium subscription model.
```

### Step 2: Add Supporting Details (1-2 hours)

**Template:**
```markdown
## Supporting Details

### Problem
[What problem do we solve? 2-3 sentences]

### Solution
[How do we solve it? 2-3 sentences]

### Market Opportunity
- **TAM:** $[X] млрд
- **SAM:** $[Y] млн
- **SOM:** $[Z] тыс (first 2 years)

### Target Segment
- **Who:** [Detailed description]
- **Size:** [X] potential customers
- **Growth:** [X]% YoY

### Business Model
- **Revenue model:** [How we make money]
- **Pricing:** [Price points]
- **Unit economics:** [CAC/LTV/payback]

### Competitive Advantage
- **Differentiation:** [How we're unique]
- **Moat:** [Sustainable advantage]

### Validation Plan
- **Hypothesis:** [What we're testing]
- **Method:** [How we'll validate]
- **Metrics:** [Success criteria]

### Timeline
- **Month 0-2:** [MVP build]
- **Month 2-6:** [Launch + validate]
- **Month 6-12:** [Scale + optimize]

### Team Needed
- **Roles:** [Who we need]
- **Timeline:** [When to hire]

### Financial Ask
- **Seed funding:** $[X]k
- **Runway:** [X] months
- **Milestone:** [What will this achieve]
```

## ✅ Output Template

```markdown
# BIG Idea Articulation

[1-page summary of the whole idea]

[The concise articulation]

## Supporting Analysis
[Problem, Solution, Market, Business Model, etc.]

## Validation Results
[What we've validated so far]

## Go-to-Market Plan
[How we'll reach customers]

[Revenue, costs, milestones]

[What could go wrong + mitigation]
```
