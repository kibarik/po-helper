# 3.1 Консолидация возможностей

## 🎯 Цель

Собрать все opportunities из 3 линз в master list.

**Время:** 1 час  
**После:** Master list из 18-35 opportunities

---

## 📝 Process

### Step 1: Aggregate Opportunities

**From each lens:**
- Linza Strategy: 5-10 opportunities
- Linza Business: 3-5 opportunities
- Linza Product: 10-20 opportunities

**Template:**
```markdown
## Master Opportunities List

### From Linza Strategy
1. **[Opportunity]:** [Description] — [Source] — [Initial CP]

### From Linza Business
...

### From Linza Product
...

**Total:** [X] opportunities
```

### Step 2: Deduplicate & Cluster

**Group similar opportunities:**
```markdown
## Opportunity Clusters

### Cluster 1: [Theme]
- [Opp 1] — [Linza source]
- [Opp 2] — [Linza source]

### Cluster 2: [Theme]
...
```

### Step 3: Preliminary Ranking

**Quick sort by:**
- Impact (potential value)
- Feasibility (can we build this?)
- Confidence (how sure are we?)

---

## ✅ Output

**Master opportunities list** ready for Confidence Point estimation
