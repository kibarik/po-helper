# 2.1 Linza Strategy — Скаутинг стратегии

## 🎯 Цель

Найти strategic opportunities через First Principles и Blue Ocean lenses.

**Время:** 4-8 часов  
**После:** 5-10 strategic opportunities с initial confidence

---

## 📋 Что такое Linza Strategy?

**Простыми словами:** Linza Strategy = смотришь на продукт с точки зрения **рынка и стратегии**, не только тактики.

**Вопросы:**
- Какая рыночная возможность?
- Как отличаемся от конкурентов?
- Какой strategic positioning?

---

## 🎯 Phase 1: First Principles Analysis (2-4 hours)

### What is First Principles?

**Простыми словами:** Thinking from fundamentals, not analogies.

**Example:**
- **Analogy thinking:** "We'll build Uber for X" (copying existing model)
- **First principles:** "What job is customer trying to do? What's the physics of the problem?"

### Step 1.1: Identify the Fundamental Problem (1 hour)

**Template:**
```markdown
## First Principles Analysis

### Question: What fundamental problem are we solving?

### Core Job (JTBD preview)
- **Functional job:** [What does customer need to do?]
- **Emotional job:** [How do they want to feel?]
- **Social job:** [How do they want to be perceived?]

### Root Cause Analysis (Why is this not solved today?)
1. **Root cause 1:** [Description]
2. **Root cause 2:** [Description]
3. **Root cause 3:** [Description]

### Ideal Solution (Physics of the problem)
- **What would ideal look like?** [Capabilities needed]
- **What prevents this today?** [Constraints]
- **What's changing that makes this possible now?** [Enablers]
```

**Пример:**
```markdown
## First Principles Analysis — AI Market Research

### Core Job
- **Functional:** Marketing teams need audience insights for campaigns
- **Emotional:** Feel confident in decisions, not guessing
- **Social:** Look data-driven to stakeholders

### Root Causes
1. **Enterprise tools too expensive:** SMBs can't afford $50k/year
2. **Too complex:** Requires PhD-level expertise to use
3. **Too slow:** Traditional research takes weeks, need real-time

### Ideal Solution
- **What:** Instant audience insights, anytime, <5 minutes
- **Prevents:** Cost (human researchers expensive), Speed (manual work slow)
- **Enablers:** AI advances (2020s), API access to social data
```

### Step 1.2: Break Down the Problem (1-2 hours)

**Decomposition:**
```markdown
## Problem Decomposition

### Sub-problems
1. **[Sub-problem 1]:** [Description] — [Solvability]
2. **[Sub-problem 2]:** [Description] — [Solvability]
3. **[Sub-problem 3]:** [Description] — [Solvability]

### Dependencies
Which sub-problems must be solved first?

### Solvability Assessment
- **Easy to solve:** [Which parts?]
- **Hard to solve:** [Which parts?]
- **Impossible today:** [Which parts? Why?]
```

## 🌊 Phase 2: Blue Ocean Strategy (2-4 hours)

### What is Blue Ocean Strategy?

**Простыми словами:** Create uncontested market space instead of competing in existing market.

**Key insight:** Don't try to be "better" than competitors — make them irrelevant.

### Step 2.1: Identify Industry Standards (1 hour)

**Template:**
```markdown
## Industry Standard Factors

List 8-10 factors that industry competes on:

1. [Factor 1]: [How it's typically done]
2. [Factor 2]: [How it's typically done]
...
10. [Factor 10]: [How it's typically done]

Example for market research tools:
1. Price: Premium ($500+/month)
2. Features: Comprehensive (200+ question types)
3. Support: High-touch (dedicated account manager)
4. Speed: Weeks to results
5. Expertise required: PhD-level
6. Data sources: Proprietary panels
7. Reporting: Advanced analytics
8. Customization: Highly customizable
9. Training: Required
10. Integration: API available
```

### Step 2.2: Apply ELIMINATE-REDUCE-RAISE-CREATE (1-2 hours)

**Template:**
```markdown
## Blue Ocean Canvas

### ELIMINATE (Which factors should be eliminated?)
- **[Factor]:** Why eliminate? [Reasoning]

### REDUCE (Which factors should be reduced below industry standard?)
- **[Factor]:** Reduce to [level]? Why? [Reasoning]

### RAISE (Which factors should be raised above industry standard?)
- **[Factor]:** Raise to [level]? Why? [Reasoning]

### CREATE (Which factors should be created that never existed?)
- **[New factor]:** What value does it create? [Reasoning]
```

**Пример:**
```markdown
## Blue Ocean Canvas — AI Market Research

### ELIMINATE
- **Account managers:** SMBs don't need/want hand-holding
- **Training:** Tool should be intuitive, not require PhD
- **Customization:** Pre-built templates > 200 options

### REDUCE
- **Price:** From $500+/mo to $49/mo (10x reduction)
- **Speed:** From weeks to minutes (1000x improvement)
- **Complexity:** From PhD-required to anyone-can-use

### RAISE
- **Automation:** From manual to AI-powered (industry first)
- **Accessibility:** From enterprise-only to SMB-friendly
- **Transparency:** From black-box to explainable AI

### CREATE
- **Instant insights:** Real-time research (never existed)
- **Self-service onboarding:** 5 minutes to first insight
- **Community data:** Aggregate anonymized data for benchmarking
```

### Step 2.3: Synthesize Strategic Positioning (1 hour)

```markdown
## Strategic Positioning Statement

**For** [target segment],
**Who** [current alternative],
**Our** [product/solution]
**Is** [new market category],
**That** [key differentiation].

**Unlike** [competitors],
**We** [primary value proposition].

**Example:**
**For** SMB marketing teams (1-100 people),
**Who** are underserved by enterprise research tools,
**Our** AI-powered market research platform
**Is** a new category of "instant insights" tools,
**That** delivers audience data in minutes, not weeks.

**Unlike** SurveyMonkey/Qualtrics (expensive, complex, slow),
**We** offer AI automation at 1/10th the cost with zero expertise required.
```

## 🎯 Phase 3: Competitive Weakness Scouting (1-2 hours)

### Step 3.1: Analyze Competitor Vulnerabilities

**Sources:**
- Customer reviews (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot)
- Social media (Twitter, Reddit complaints)
- Industry forums

**Template:**
```markdown
## Competitor Weakness Analysis

### [Competitor Name]

### Customer Complaints (Top 5)
1. **[Complaint]:** [Frequency] — [Severity]
   - **Root cause:** [Why does this exist?]
   - **Our opportunity:** [How can we solve this?]

2. **[Complaint]:** [Frequency] — [Severity]
   ...

### Vulnerability Assessment
- **High-impact vulnerabilities:** [Which complaints matter most?]
- **Unaddressed by competitor:** [Which problems are they ignoring?]
- **Feasible for us:** [Which can we realistically solve?]
```
