# 6.2 Pre-mortem Analysis

## 🎯 Цель

Провести risk analysis перед final sign-off.

**Время:** 1-2 hours  
**После:** Risk mitigation plan + final go-ahead

---

## 📋 Что такое Pre-mortem?

**Простыми словами:** Pre-mortem = "предсмертный анализ" — спрашиваем "Что могло пойти не так через 6 месяцев?"

**Отличие от Post-mortem:**
- **Post-mortem:** Что пошло не так? (после провала)
- **Pre-mortem:** Что МОГЛО пойти не так? (до начала)

---

## 📝 Process

### Step 1: Brainstorm Risks (30 min)

**Question:** "What could go wrong in 6 months?"

**Categories:**

#### Market Risks
```
- Competitor launches similar solution
- Market need not validated at scale
- Pricing resistance beyond early adopters
- Regulatory changes
```

#### Product Risks
```
- Technical execution harder than estimated
- Features don't deliver expected value
- Scalability issues
- Quality problems
```

#### Team Risks
```
- Key person leaves
- Skills gap
- Communication breakdown
```

#### Business Risks
```
- CAC higher than projected
- LTV lower than projected
- Churn higher than expected
- Runway shorter than planned
```

### Step 2: Assess Risks (30 min)

**For each risk:**

```markdown
## Risk: [Name]

### Likelihood
**Probability:** [Low/Medium/High]
**Why:** [Reasoning]

### Impact
**Severity:** [Low/Medium/High]
**What happens:** [Consequences]

### Early Warning Signals
- **Signal 1:** [What to monitor]
- **Signal 2:** [What to monitor]

### Mitigation Plan
- **Preventive:** [What to do now]
- **Reactive:** [What to do if happens]
```

### Step 3: Create Risk Matrix (30 min)

```markdown
## Risk Matrix

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Priority | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|----------|------------|
| [Risk 1] | High | High | 🔴 Critical | [Plan] |
| [Risk 2] | Medium | High | 🟡 High | [Plan] |
| [Risk 3] | Low | Medium | 🟢 Medium | [Plan] |

**Priority breakdown:**
- 🔴 **Critical:** High likelihood + High impact → Address immediately
- 🟡 **High:** Either High likelihood OR High impact → Monitor closely
- 🟢 **Medium:** Lower likelihood/impact → Accept
```

### Step 4: Define Monitoring (15 min)

**For critical/high risks:**

```markdown
## Risk Monitoring Plan

### [Risk 1]
- **Metric to track:** [What to measure]
- **Frequency:** [How often to check]
- **Owner:** [Who monitors]
- **Trigger:** [What action if metric crosses threshold]

### [Risk 2]
...
```

### Step 5: Final Go/No-Go Decision (15 min)

**Questions:**
1. Are critical risks mitigated?
2. Is risk level acceptable?
3. Do we have contingency plans?

**Decision:**
```markdown
## Go/No-Go Decision

**Risks assessed:** [X] total
- **Critical:** [X] — [All/Most] mitigated ✅
- **High:** [X] — [Monitoring plan in place] ✅
- **Medium:** [X] — [Acceptable] ✅

**Decision:** ✅ **GO** / ❌ **NO-GO**

**Rationale:**
[Why this decision]

**Conditions:**
- [Condition 1]: [Must be true]
- [Condition 2]: [Must be true]

**Review checkpoint:**
[When to reassess risks]
```

---

## ✅ Output

**Risk register** + **Monitoring plan** + **Final go/no-go decision**

---

## 🎯 Success Criteria

✅ 10-20 risks identified  
✅ Critical risks have mitigation plans  
✅ Monitoring plan defined  
✅ Go/no-go decision made  
✅ Team aligned on risks
