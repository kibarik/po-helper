# 6.1 Pitching of Trust

## 🎯 Цель

Получить stakeholder approval и resources.

**Время:** 2-4 hours (prep) + 1 hour (meeting)  
**После:** Bunch approved + resources committed

---

## 📋 Что такое Pitching of Trust?

**Простыми словами:** Pitching of Trust = презентация команде, где они "пытаются завалить" твою идею.

**Цель:** Назначить **final Confidence Points** командой, не одним человеком.

---

## 📝 Process

### Step 1: Prepare Pitch (1-2 hours)

**Based on [[5.2 Bunch Presentation]], create:**

1. **10-minute pitch** (not full presentation, focused)
   - Context (3 min)
   - BIG Idea (2 min)
   - Bunch overview (3 min)
   - Validation results (2 min)

2. **Q&A prep** (anticipate questions)
   - Why these features?
   - What if validation fails?
   - What's Plan B?

### Step 2: Schedule Pitching Session (30 min)

**Invite:**
- Product team (must-have)
- Stakeholders (if needed)
- Technical lead (if available)

**Duration:** 60-90 minutes

### Step 3: Conduct Pitching (60-90 min)

**Format:**

```markdown
## Pitching Agenda

### 1. Context Presentation (3 min)
- Market insights
- Current state
- Vision

### 2. BIG Idea Articulation (2 min)
- Problem/solution
- Target segment
- Differentiation

### 3. Bunch Overview (3 min)
- 5-7 features
- Confidence Points
- mNSM estimates

### 4. Validation Results (2 min)
- What validated
- How validated
- Results

### 5. Q&A — "Try to Kill It" (20-30 min)
**Team asks skeptical questions:**
- "What if [risk] happens?"
- "Why not [alternative approach]?"
- "What evidence do you have?"
- "What's the worst case?"

**Your role:** Answer honestly, acknowledge unknowns

### 6. Confidence Point Assignment (15 min)
**For each Bunch item:**
- Team discusses
- Team votes on CP (1-10)
- Items with CP <5 dropped
- Items with CP ≥5 approved

### 7. Resource Commitment (10 min)
- What's needed to execute?
- Budget approval
- Timeline agreement

### 8. Summary (5 min)
- Approved Bunch: [List]
- Assigned CPs: [List]
- Resources: [What committed]
- Timeline: [When starts]
```

### Step 4: Document Outcomes (30 min)

**Template:**
```markdown
## Pitching of Trust Results

**Date:** [Date]
**Attendees:** [List]

### Approved Bunch

| Feature | Initial CP | Final CP | Decision |
|---------|------------|----------|----------|
| [Feature 1] | [X]/10 | [Y]/10 | ✅ Approved |
| [Feature 2] | [X]/10 | [Y]/10 | ✅ Approved |
| [Feature 3] | [X]/10 | [Y]/10 | ❌ Dropped (CP too low) |

### Resources Committed
- **Budget:** $[X]k approved
- **Team:** [Roles/hiring]
- **Timeline:** [Start date] → [Delivery date]

### Conditions
- **Dependencies:** [What must happen first]
- **Checkpoints:** [When to review progress]
- **Success criteria:** [What defines success]

### Action Items
1. **[Action 1]:** [Owner] — [Due date]
2. **[Action 2]:** [Owner] — [Due date]
```

---

## 🎯 Success Criteria

✅ Bunch approved (5-7 features)  
✅ Final CP assigned by team  
✅ Resources committed  
✅ Timeline agreed  
✅ Action items documented
