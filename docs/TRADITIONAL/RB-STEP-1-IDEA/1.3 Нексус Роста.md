# 1.3 Нексус Роста — Полная инструкция

## 🎯 Цель

Создать **Нексус Роста** — понимание бизнес-модели и юнит-экономики.

**Время:** 1-2 часа  
**После:** Базовая бизнес-модель + unit economics понятны

---

## 📋 Что такое Нексус Роста?

**Простыми словами:** Нексус Роста = "финансовая модель" твоего бизнеса — как зарабатываешь деньги и можешь ли масштабироваться.

---

## 🎯 3 Компонента

### 1. Business Model (Как зарабатываем?)
### 2. Unit Economics (Работает ли на юните?)
### 3. Growth Levers (Как масштабируем?)

---

## 📝 Phase 1: Business Model (30-45 min)

### Step 1.1: Revenue Model

**Вопросы:**
1. Как мы зарабатываем? (subscription, transaction, marketplace, etc.)
2. Как мы взимаем плату? (monthly, annually, per-use, etc.)
3. Какие pricing tiers?

**Template:**
```markdown
## Revenue Model

### Pricing Strategy
- Model: [Subscription/Transaction/Marketplace/Other]
- Tiers: [List pricing tiers]
- Billing: [Monthly/Annual/Per-use]

### Example:
### Pricing Strategy
- Model: Freemium Subscription
- Tiers:
  - Free: 5 searches/month
  - Pro: $49/month (100 searches)
  - Business: $199/month (unlimited + API)
- Billing: Monthly (annual 20% discount)
```

### Step 1.2: Customer Acquisition

**Как привлекаем клиентов:**
```markdown
## Customer Acquisition

### Channels (Priority order)
1. [Channel 1]: [Est. cost/conversion]
2. [Channel 2]: [Est. cost/conversion]

### Example:
1. Content marketing (SEO blog posts): 200 leads/mo, $50 CAC
2. Referrals: 50 leads/mo, $0 CAC
3. Paid ads: 100 leads/mo, $150 CAC
```

## 🎯 Phase 2: Unit Economics (30-45 min)

### Step 2.1: Calculate Key Metrics

**Template:**
```markdown
## Unit Economics

### CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **Calculation:** [Total acquisition spend] / [New customers]
- **Value:** $[X]
- **Target:** <$[Y]

### LTV (Lifetime Value)
- **Calculation:** [ARPU] × [Customer lifetime in months]
- **Value:** $[X]
- **Target:** >$[Y]

### Payback Period
- **Calculation:** CAC / (Monthly revenue per customer)
- **Value:** [X] months
- **Target:** <12 months

### Gross Margin
- **Calculation:** (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue
- **Value:** [X]%
- **Target:** >70% for SaaS

### Key Ratio
- **LTV:CAC:** [X]:1 (Target: ≥3:1)
```

**Пример:**
```markdown
## Unit Economics (Example)

### CAC
- **Calculation:** $12,000 / 100 customers = $120
- **Target:** <$150

### LTV
- **Calculation:** $50/month × 14 months = $700
- **Target:** >$500

### Payback Period
- **Calculation:** $120 / $50 = 2.4 months
- **Target:** <6 months

### Gross Margin
- **Calculation:** ($50 - $10) / $50 = 80%
- **Target:** >70%

### Key Ratio
- **LTV:CAC:** $700:$120 = 5.8:1 ✅ (Target ≥3:1)
```

## 🎯 Phase 3: Growth Levers (30 min)

### Step 3.1: Identify Growth Levers

**3 типа рычагов:**

#### 1. Acquisition Levers (Привлечение)
```markdown
## Acquisition Levers
- [Lever 1]: [Potential impact]
- [Lever 2]: [Potential impact]

Example:
## Acquisition Levers
- SEO content (blog posts): +100 leads/mo
- Product-led growth (viral loops): +200 leads/mo
- Partnerships: +50 leads/mo
```

#### 2. Retention Levers (Удержание)
```markdown
## Retention Levers
- [Lever 1]: [Potential impact]
- [Lever 2]: [Potential impact]

Example:
## Retention Levers
- Onboarding optimization: +15% retention
- Feature notifications: +10% engagement
- Community building: +20% LTV
```

#### 3. Monetization Levers (Монетизация)
```markdown
## Monetization Levers
- [Lever 1]: [Potential impact]
- [Lever 2]: [Potential impact]

Example:
## Monetization Levers
- Upsell to Business tier: +$20/mo per customer
- Annual prepay: +20% revenue upfront
- Add-on features: +$10/mo attach rate
```

## 🚨 Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Не могу рассчитать CAC/LTV (пока нет клиентов)"

**Solution:** Используй estimates based on:
- Industry benchmarks (SaaS CAC usually $50-500)
- Similar companies
- Pre-launch estimates (mark as preliminary)

### Problem: "LTV:CAC <3:1 (unprofitable)"

**Solution:**
- Reduce CAC (cheaper acquisition channels)
- Increase LTV (better retention, upsells)
- Increase pricing
- Accept lower ratio initially (improve over time)
