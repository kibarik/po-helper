# 2.3 Linza Product — Скаутинг продукта

## 🎯 Цель

Найти product opportunities через Jobs To Be Done и Design Thinking.

**Время:** 4-12 часов (включая интервью)  
**После:** 10-20 product opportunities с customer context

---

## 📋 Что такое Linza Product?

**Простыми словами:** Linza Product = смотришь на продукт с точки зрения **customer needs and value creation**.

**Вопросы:**
- Какие jobs выполняем для клиентов?
- Какую ценность создаем?
- Какое решение предлагаем?

---

## 🎯 Phase 1: Jobs To Be Done Research (3-8 hours)

### What is JTBD?

**Ключевой инсайт:** Люди не покупают продукты, они "нанимают" их для выполнения работ.

**3 типа Jobs:**
1. **Functional Jobs:** Что делают?
2. **Emotional Jobs:** Как чувствуют?
3. **Social Jobs:** Как выглядят?

### Step 1.1: Conduct JTBD Interviews (2-6 hours)

**Target:** 5-10 interviews (30 min each)

**Interview Guide Template:**

```markdown
## JTBD Interview Guide

### Opening (2 min)
"Thanks for taking time. I'm researching how [target role] handles [problem area]. No right/wrong answers, just your experience. This will take 30 minutes, OK?"

### Context Setting (5 min)
"Walk me through the last time you [problem scenario]?"
- [When did this happen?]
- [What was the situation?]
- [Who else was involved?]

### Problem Exploration (10 min)
"What made that challenging?"
- [What was difficult?]
- [What did you try first?]
- [What worked/didn't work?]

### Workarounds (5 min)
"How do you handle this today?"
- [What tools/solutions do you use?]
- [What workarounds have you created?]
- [What do you wish existed?]

### Emotional & Social (5 min)
"How does this make you feel?"
- [Frustrations?]
- [Anxieties?]
- [What would make you feel [emotion]?]

### Ideal Solution (5 min)
"What would ideal look like?"
- [What capabilities would it have?]
- [How would it work?]
- [What would you pay for?]

### Closing (3 min)
"Any other thoughts on this topic? Can I follow up with clarifying questions?"

**Tips:**
- Record interviews (with permission)
- Take detailed notes
- Focus on stories, not opinions
- Ask "why?" 5 times (root cause)
```

### Step 1.2: Analyze Interviews (1-2 hours)

**Template:**
```markdown
## JTBD Analysis

### Jobs Identified

#### Functional Jobs
1. **[Job 1]:** [Description]
   - **When happens:** [Trigger events]
   - **Frequency:** [How often?]
   - **Importance:** [1-10]

#### Emotional Jobs
1. **[Job 1]:** [Description]
   - **When felt:** [Situations]
   - **Intensity:** [1-10]

#### Social Jobs
1. **[Job 1]:** [Description]
   - **Audience:** [Whose opinion matters?]
   - **Importance:** [1-10]

### Segmentation by "Hiring Criteria"

#### Segment A: "[Name]"
- **When they hire:** [Trigger events]
- **What they fire:** [Current alternatives]
- **Success criteria:** [What matters?]
- **Quote:** [Real customer quote]

#### Segment B: "[Name]"
...

**Example:**

#### Segment A: "Newbies"
- **When they hire:** Starting new marketing role, no prior experience
- **What they fire:** Guessing, gut feel, expensive agencies
- **Success criteria:** Look competent to boss, learn quickly
- **Quote:** "I was drowning in data but had no insights. Needed something fast to prove I know what I'm doing."

#### Segment B: "Efficiency Seekers"
- **When they hire:** Under deadline, need results fast
- **What they fire:** Time-consuming traditional research
- **Success criteria:** Impress stakeholders with quick insights
- **Quote:** "Boss needs slides by EOD. Can't wait 2 weeks for a survey."
```

## 🎨 Phase 2: Design Thinking Problem Discovery (1-4 hours)

### What is Design Thinking?

**5 стадий:**
1. **Empathize:** Понять пользователей
2. **Define:** Сформулировать проблему
3. **Ideate:** Генерировать решения
4. **Prototype:** Создать прототип
5. **Test:** Проверить с пользователями

**Здесь фокус на Empathize + Define**

### Step 2.1: Empathy Building (1-2 hours)

**Methods:**

#### Option A: Customer Interviews (если не делал в JTBD)
- 5-8 interviews
- Focus on stories, not opinions
- Observe (если возможно) how they work

#### Option B: Secondary Research
- Reddit/forums (customer complaints)
- Social media (pain points)
- Competitor reviews (1-star reviews = gold mine)

**Output:** Empathy Map

```markdown
## Empathy Map — [Segment Name]

### Says (What do they say?)
- "[Quote 1]"
- "[Quote 2]"

### Thinks (What might they think?)
- [Belief 1]
- [Belief 2]

### Does (What do they do?)
- [Behavior 1]
- [Behavior 2]

### Feels (What emotions?)
- [Emotion 1]
- [Emotion 2]

### Pains (Frustrations?)
- [Pain 1]
- [Pain 2]

### Gains (What would delight them?)
- [Gain 1]
- [Gain 2]
```

### Step 2.2: Problem Statement Articulation (1-2 hours)

**Template:**
```markdown
## Problem Statements

### Problem Statement 1
"[User type] has [problem]
when [context/situation]
because [root cause].

How might we [direction of solution]?"

**Example:**
"Marketing managers have ineffective audience targeting
when launching new campaigns
because they rely on gut feel vs real data.

How might we give them rapid, affordable audience insights?"

### Problem Statement 2
...
```

**Tips:**
- One problem statement per segment
- Focus on root cause, not symptoms
- Make HMW (How Might We) directional, not prescriptive

## 🎯 Phase 3: Ideation (1-2 hours)

### Step 3.1: Generate Solution Ideas

**Methods:**

#### Method 1: Brainwriting (6-3-5 technique)
```
1. Each person writes 6 ideas in 5 minutes (silent)
2. Pass paper to next person
3. Build on ideas (add 3 more in 5 minutes)
4. Repeat until paper returns to origin
5. Discuss and select top ideas
```

#### Method 2: SCAMPER (для incremental tweaks)
```
- **S**ubstitute: Что можно заменить?
- **C**ombine: Что можно объединить?
- **A**dapt: Что можно адаптировать?
- **M**odify: Что можно изменить?
- **P**ut to other use: Другое применение?
- **E**liminate: Что убрать?
- **R**everse: Сделать наоборот?
```

#### Method 3: Reverse Brainstorming
```
"How could we CREATE this problem?"
→ Invert to find solutions
```

**Rules:**
- Quantity over quality (aim 50+ ideas)
- No judgment during generation
- Build on others' ideas
- Wild ideas encouraged

### Step 3.2: Cluster and Select (30 min)

**Group ideas into themes:**
```markdown
## Idea Clusters

### Theme 1: [Name]
- Idea 1
- Idea 2
- Idea 3

### Theme 2: [Name]
...

## Top Ideas (for Bunch consideration)
1. **[Idea 1]:** [Description] — [Confidence 1-10]
2. **[Idea 2]:** [Description] — [Confidence 1-10]
...
```
