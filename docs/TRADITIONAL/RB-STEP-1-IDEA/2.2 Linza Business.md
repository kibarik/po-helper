# 2.2 Linza Business — Скаутинг бизнеса

## 🎯 Цель

Найти business model opportunities через BMC и Lean Canvas lenses.

**Время:** 2-4 часа  
**После:** 3-5 business opportunities

---

## 📝 Phase 1: Business Model Canvas Analysis (1-2 hours)

### Step 1.1: Map Current Industry Business Models

**For each BMC building block, ask:**

```markdown
## [Building Block] Analysis

### Industry Standard
- **Current approach:** [How industry does this]
- **Problems:** [What's broken?]

### Opportunity
- **Gap:** [What's missing?]
- **Innovation:** [How could we do this differently?]
- **Impact:** [Potential value?]
```

**Example (Revenue Streams):**
```markdown
## Revenue Streams Analysis

### Industry Standard
- **Current:** Subscription $50-500/month, annual contracts
- **Problems:** High barrier for SMBs, locked-in yearly

### Opportunity
- **Gap:** No flexible, SMB-friendly pricing
- **Innovation:** Monthly subscriptions, freemium, usage-based
- **Impact:** 10x lower barrier to entry
```

---

## 🎯 Phase 2: Lean Canvas Business Model Gaps (1-2 hours)

### Step 2.1: Identify Problem-Solution Gaps

**Template:**
```markdown
## Problem-Solution Gap Analysis

### Top 3 Customer Problems (from JTBD)
1. **[Problem 1]:** [Frequency] — [Severity]
   - **Current solutions:** [What exists?]
   - **Gaps:** [What's missing?]
   - **Our opportunity:** [How could we solve better?]
```

---

## 🎯 Phase 3: Monetization Mechanics Ideation (1 hour)

**Template:**
```markdown
## Monetization Opportunities

### [Opportunity Name]
- **Current approach:** [How industry monetizes]
- **Innovation:** [Our different approach]
- **Why works:** [Customer behavior insight]
- **Revenue impact:** [Potential uplift]
```

**Пример:**
```markdown
## Freemium Opportunity

### Current approach
- **Industry:** Enterprise tools only, no free tier
- **Barrier:** $500+/month minimum

### Innovation
- **Our approach:** Freemium with generous free tier
- **Why works:** Low-risk trial, viral growth
- **Revenue impact:** 5-10% free → paid conversion standard
```
